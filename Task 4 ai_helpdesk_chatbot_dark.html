2

3
TF-IDF vectorization
Transform cleaned tokens into feature vectors. Vocabulary trained on FAQ dataset of 142 entries.
4
Intent classification
Logistic Regression classifier picks top intent. Confidence <0.45 triggers fallback "I don't understand" response.
