TF-IDF vectorization explained
TF — term frequency
How often a word appears in the document. Normalised by total word count.
TF = count(t) / total_words
IDF — inverse doc frequency
How rare a word is across all documents. Rare words carry more signal.
IDF = log(N / df(t))
